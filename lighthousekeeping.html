Light Housekeeping

Comfort and security are an important facet of life especially for older adults who age at home. Generally, the elderly suffer from various ailments as they advance in age and because of this, they find it difficult to perform normal everyday tasks. Many people turn to family members for assistance with daily chores. However, not all family members have the time to take on these additional tasks and in fact would prefer to spend quality time with their loved ones.

What it is: Our home care services are designed to ensure you can spend quality time with your family while our highly skilled caregivers can assist you with day to day chores like laundry, vacuuming, cleaning and so on.
