About Us

Learn About Company

Abefal Global Home Care is an organization full of heart.

We believe in creating special moments for our clients each and every day, whether at the bedside or on the phone. These moments touch the lives of clients and our employees, and they guide the way we run our business. These moments inspire, change and encourage us. They are what we call the Abefal global homecare difference. We are dedicated in enhancing the quality of life, dignity and independence of all Ghanaians and globally, by providing customized care plans and solutions that allow clients to remain in the comfort of their homes.

PURPOSE

To bring Hope and Happiness to the community

MISSION

We share our knowledge, provide the best care and help each client to realize their most meaningful goals for health and well-being.

VALUES

- Passion - Love - Work with purpose - Integrity and Honesty - Collaboration and teamwork - Respect and dignity

VISION

To forever impact and create a compassionate environment for each person entrusted to our care and to inspire hope and healing by helping those individuals achieve their goals and highest level of physical, emotional and spiritual well-being.
